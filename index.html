✦ Features

Everything you need to manage money

Built on proper double-entry accounting. Designed for the everyday Bangladeshi who wants to save more and stress less.

📊

Double-Entry Accounting

Every transaction records both where money comes from and where it goes — proper accounting, not just a note-keeping app.

AI
🤖

Smart Budget AI

AI analyses your last 3 months and suggests an optimal budget at the start of each month. Accept in one tap or customise it.

👛

Multi-Wallet Management

Cash, bank, debit card, credit card, loans to/from others — all in one place with live balance tracking and overdraft protection.

📈

Rich Reports & Charts

Daily, weekly, monthly and yearly reports. Bar charts, doughnut charts, trend lines and head-wise filtering with PDF export.

🌐

Bengali & English

Full bilingual support. Switch any time. Bengali is the default. Every label, button and message translates instantly.

🔄

Offline Sync

Works fully offline. Auto-syncs across all devices when online. Daily auto-backup to your own Google Drive — free.

🎯

Budget vs Actual

Colour-coded progress bars show exactly how close each category is to its budget limit — with AI-generated suggestions.

🏆

Savings Milestones

Monthly savings celebrated with a congratulations popup. Yearly summary shows every month's savings at a glance.

🔒

Asset Protection

Wallets can never go negative. Overdraft and same-wallet transfer are blocked automatically — proper accounting enforced.
